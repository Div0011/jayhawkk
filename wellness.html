Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Wellness and Health services

What does Jayhawkk's Wellness and Health support include?

Our Wellness and Health support focuses on the complete wellbeing of each person ‚Äî physically, emotionally, and socially. This includes nutrition and hydration, gentle exercise, emotional companionship, mental stimulation, and regular health monitoring.

Who can benefit from Jayhawkk's Wellness and Health services?

Our services are designed for individuals of all ages who may need support to maintain a healthy and independent lifestyle at home ‚Äî including older adults, people living with disabilities, or anyone recovering from illness or hospital stays.

How does Jayhawkk promote emotional wellbeing?

We understand the importance of emotional balance. Our carers offer companionship, active listening, and uplifting daily interactions to help reduce loneliness, boost confidence, and create a sense of comfort and belonging.

Does Jayhawkk assist with healthy meal planning and nutrition?

Yes. Our carers can help prepare nutritious meals, ensure proper hydration, and support dietary preferences or medical needs ‚Äî all while encouraging a healthy and balanced diet.

What kind of physical or exercise support is available?

We assist with light exercise, stretching, and mobility routines suitable for each person's ability level. The goal is to improve movement, balance, and overall strength safely and effectively.

How does Jayhawkk monitor health and wellbeing at home?

Our team keeps a close eye on changes in mood, appetite, energy levels, and general health. We also communicate regularly with families, GPs, and other professionals to ensure continuity of care and early intervention when needed.

Can Jayhawkk help with cognitive or memory support?

Absolutely. We engage clients in mentally stimulating activities such as puzzles, reminiscence sessions, reading, or music therapy to support memory, concentration, and mental agility.

How does Jayhawkk ensure a holistic approach to wellness?

We see every person as unique. Our holistic care combines physical support, emotional wellbeing, and social connection ‚Äî creating a balanced, person-centred experience that nurtures overall health and happiness.

Is social engagement part of the Wellness and Health plan?

Yes. We encourage clients to stay socially active ‚Äî whether by chatting with loved ones, pursuing hobbies, or joining local community events. Social connection is vital to maintaining a positive and fulfilling life.

How can I arrange Wellness and Health support with Jayhawkk?

You can reach us through our website's Contact Page or call our friendly team. We'll schedule a free care assessment to understand your needs and design a personalised care plan that supports your wellbeing at home.
