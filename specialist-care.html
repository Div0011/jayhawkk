Specialist Care:

Skilled support for complex needs, delivered with dignity and compassion.

At Jayhawkk Care Ltd, we understand that no two individuals are the same—especially when managing complex health conditions. That’s why we focus on delivering care that is as unique as the people we support.

Our dedicated care professionals are trained in both clinical and personal care, ensuring every service is delivered with skill, empathy, and attention to detail. Working closely with healthcare teams, families, and local authorities, we develop personalised care plans that reflect each person’s medical, emotional, and lifestyle needs.

From day-to-day support to specialised clinical care, we aim to **empower those we care for**—encouraging independence, upholding dignity, and enhancing overall quality of life.

Specialist Care Services Include:

Dementia & Alzheimer's Care

Compassionate cognitive-support care to promote safety, familiarity, and wellbeing.

Physical Disability Support

Empowering individuals to live independently with person-centred care.

Sensory Impairment Support

Support for individuals with hearing or visual impairments.

Medication Administration Support

Trained support with medication administration, MAR charts, and compliance.

Catheter & Stoma Care

Trained support following clinical care plans and infection-control standards.

Diabetes Care & Monitoring

Blood sugar monitoring, insulin reminders (no injection unless nurse trained).

Falls Prevention & Mobility Management

Risk assessment, mobility aid support, and fall-prevention routines.

Live-in Specialist Support

Intensive round-the-clock care tailored to unique medical/social needs.

Arthritis Care

Support with mobility, joint-friendly routines, and pain-aware assistance to promote comfort.
