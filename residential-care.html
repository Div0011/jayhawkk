Residential Care

Designed to deliver personalised, high-quality care where it matters most—at home.

Get In Touch

Residential care - In the comfort of your own home

Residential care - In the comfort of your own home. At Jayhawkk, we specialize in delivering high-quality residential care support as part of our comprehensive domiciliary care offerings. Our core mission is to provide compassionate and person-centred care that enables individuals to maintain their independence, safety, and comfort within their own homes.

We recognize that every individual has unique needs. That’s why our services are fully tailored—whether a client requires 24-hour care or support for just a few hours a day. Jayhawkk collaborates closely with clients and their families to design bespoke care plans that uphold dignity, promote well-being, and enhance overall quality of life.

“We care for elderly people”

Our Residential Care Support Includes

Personal Care Assistance

We assist with essential daily activities such as washing, dressing, grooming, and toileting—always delivered in a respectful and sensitive manner.

Meal Preparation

Whether you need support for just an hour or several visits per day, we offer flexible scheduling to suit your lifestyle and preferences.

Medication Reminders

We help with reminders and supervision of prescribed medications to keep routines safe and consistent.

Mobility Support

Support with moving around the home safely, including assistance with walking, transfers, and using mobility aids.

Household Tasks

Help with everyday tasks like laundry, light cleaning, and keeping the living environment tidy and comfortable.

Companionship

Our carers offer meaningful companionship—whether it’s sharing a chat, reading, or enjoying a favourite activity together.

Morning and Evening Routines

We provide assistance with starting the day or getting ready for bed, ensuring comfort and peace of mind.

Flexible Care Visits

Our care plans range from short daily visits to extended support, based entirely on what each client requires.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Residential Care services

Who can access Jayhawkk’s Residential Care Support?

Our services are available to older adults, individuals recovering from illness or surgery, and anyone needing extra help due to disability or health conditions.

How flexible are the care schedules at Jayhawkk?

We offer flexible care visits tailored to your needs, from brief check-ins to multiple visits per day, including morning and evening routines.

Is companionship part of Jayhawkk’s care approach?

Our carers offer meaningful companionship—whether it’s sharing a chat, reading, or enjoying a favourite activity together, promoting emotional well-being.

How does Jayhawkk ensure quality and safety?

All carers undergo thorough training, background checks, and ongoing supervision. Jayhawkk creates personalised care plans and regularly reviews them with clients and families.

How can I start receiving Residential Care Support from Jayhawkk?

Simply contact Jayhawkk Domiciliary Care Agency to arrange a free assessment. Our team will work with you to design a care plan tailored to your specific needs.
