About Us

Jayhawkk Ltd is a dedicated domiciliary personal care provider committed to delivering safe, compassionate, and person-centred care to individuals in the comfort of their own homes. Based in Coventry, West Midlands, our service supports adults who require assistance with daily living tasks, personal care, medication management, emotional support, and maintaining independence within their familiar environment.

Our care philosophy upholds dignity, respect, privacy, and choice as fundamental principles. Every care package is tailored to the unique needs, preferences, and goals of each service user, ensuring consistent, high-quality support that promotes wellbeing and enhances quality of life.

Jayhawkk Ltd operates in full alignment with the Care Quality Commission (CQC) standards, including the five key measures of Safe, Effective, Caring, Responsive, and Well-Led care. Our team of trained professionals is committed to safeguarding each individual, fostering trust, transparency, and open communication with families and healthcare partners, and delivering care in accordance with best practice, professional codes, and ethical conduct.

Our vision is to be recognised as a reliable and compassionate care provider that empowers individuals to live independently, safely, and confidently at home. Through continuous improvement, robust governance, and a culture of excellence, Jayhawkk Ltd strives to exceed expectations and deliver outstanding home-care services across the community we serve.

Specialist Care:

Skilled support for complex needs, delivered with dignity and compassion.

At Jayhawkk Care Ltd, we understand that no two individuals are the same—especially when managing complex health conditions. That’s why we focus on delivering care that is as unique as the people we support.

Our dedicated care professionals are trained in both clinical and personal care, ensuring every service is delivered with skill, empathy, and attention to detail. Working closely with healthcare teams, families, and local authorities, we develop personalised care plans that reflect each person’s medical, emotional, and lifestyle needs.

From day-to-day support to specialised clinical care, we aim to empower those we care for—encouraging independence, upholding dignity, and enhancing overall quality of life.

Residential care - In the comfort of your own home

Residential care - In the comfort of your own home. At Jayhawkk, we specialize in delivering high-quality residential care support as part of our comprehensive domiciliary care offerings. Our core mission is to provide compassionate and person-centred care that enables individuals to maintain their independence, safety, and comfort within their own homes.

We recognize that every individual has unique needs. That’s why our services are fully tailored—whether a client requires 24-hour care or support for just a few hours a day. Jayhawkk collaborates closely with clients and their families to design bespoke care plans that uphold dignity, promote well-being, and enhance overall quality of life.
