Our Mission

At Jayhawkk Ltd, our mission is to deliver safe, compassionate, and person-centred care that nurtures the whole individual—mind, body, and emotional wellbeing. We provide care with integrity, dignity, and transparency, ensuring every decision is rooted in respect and ethical practice. Through mindful caregiving, holistic wellbeing approaches, and meaningful human connection, we empower people to live healthier, happier, and more independent lives in the comfort of their own homes.

Our vision

• ⁠Integrity in Action- we do the right thing everytime transparent, safe and compliant practice guided by cqc standards and continuous improvement and we focus totally on compassionate care, dignity and respect.

•⁠ Mindful Caregiving - we provide care with calm presence, deep listening and full awareness ensuring every interaction is respectful, compassionate and person centred.

•⁠ Holistic Wellbeing - we support the whole person with body, mind and emotional health through personalised care, mini-yoga, meditation, positive routines, and meaningful engagement.
